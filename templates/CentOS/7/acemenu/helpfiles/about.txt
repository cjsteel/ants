About acemenu
-------------

acemenu is a terminal (command line) based learning system to introduce
terminal base commands and options.

Usage
=====

To invoke the acemenu at any time type `menu` in your terminal session.

The **clear** Command
=====================
 
Entering `clear` (without the back ticks) at the terminal command prompt clears
the terminal of commands and output.
